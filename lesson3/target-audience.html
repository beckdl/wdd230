Target Audience

The targeted audience for this website are those that are wanting to find information regarding the businesses within the local area of the Bridger Valley.

Personas

A person living in the area that is covered by the town or district.

Person moving into area or visiting.

Scenarios

  1. A visitor to the area trying to find local businesses to see what is able to be purchased.
  2. A prospective business owner who would like to find information on how to start their business in this new area.
  3. A person looking to join the Chamber of Commerce to help their community.
  4. A person doing a project on a period of time within the city.
  5. A person curious about the local happenings and information.
